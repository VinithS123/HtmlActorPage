Vijay

Vijay, whose full name is Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, is an Indian actor, dancer, playback singer, and philanthropist. He works predominantly in Tamil cinema and is referred to by fans and media as "Thalapathy" (commander). Born on June 22, 1974 in Chennai, India, he is one of the highest paid actors in Tamil cinema. Vijay is known for his acting, singing, and dancing skills, and has a fan following not only in India but also in China, Japan, UK, and France.
